About Us

Orphancare is an organization that want
to raise awareness in relation to SDG,
Goal 1: No Poverty. We are focusing in
helping the orphans in Malaysia. We
would like to help them in terms of
food, clothes, and education.

We also provide platform for
the public to donate the orphans.
We are a 100% trusted organization.
We will not use the money, food or
clothes donated by the public to other purpose.
